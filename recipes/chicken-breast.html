Garlic Knot Chicken Breast

Garlic Knot Chicken is a flavorful, pan-seared chicken dish coated in a buttery garlic sauce, topped with Parmesan and fresh parsley, and served with optional lemon wedges for a bright, zesty finish.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided
  • 4 chicken cutlets (about 1 1/2 pounds)
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt, plus more to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 large cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup packed fresh parsley leaves and tender stems, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup finely grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil and 2 tablespoons of butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Pat the chicken dry and season with 1 teaspoon salt and Â½ teaspoon pepper.
  3. Cook the chicken until golden brown and fully cooked, about 3 minutes per side, then transfer to a plate.
  4. Off the heat, melt the remaining butter in the skillet, add garlic, and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  5. Return the chicken and its juices to the skillet, add half of the parsley, and toss to coat; season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve topped with Parmesan and the remaining parsley, with lemon wedges on the side if desired.
